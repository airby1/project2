The History

House Slytherin has the most prestigious history of all the houses. Our founder Salazar Slytherin created the house with the four other founders. Unlike the others Slytherin believed that only pure bloods belonged in Hogtwarts. The other founders didn't agree with Salazar's views and after an argument he left the school. Despite this Slytherin is still a powerfull house in Hogwarts.
